<p><p>The electric vehicle (EV) charging equipment market primarily consists of two types: AC charging piles and DC charging piles. AC charging piles supply alternating current and are commonly used for home and public charging stations, offering slower charging speeds usually suited for overnight or longer-duration charging. In contrast, DC charging piles convert electrical current to direct current, enabling rapid charging, which is ideal for commercial locations and highway stations, allowing EVs to recharge significantly faster, typically in 30 minutes or less.</p></p>
