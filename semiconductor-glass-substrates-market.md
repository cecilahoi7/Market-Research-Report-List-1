<p><p>The semiconductor glass substrates market is primarily divided into alkali glass and alkali-free glass types. Alkali glass contains sodium and potassium, offering good thermal stability and cost-effectiveness, making it suitable for various applications. In contrast, alkali-free glass minimizes the presence of alkali metals, enhancing thermal stability and electrical performance, which is crucial for advanced semiconductor technologies. Each type caters to specific performance requirements, influencing their adoption in different semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
