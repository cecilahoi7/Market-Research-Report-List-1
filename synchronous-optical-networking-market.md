<p><p>The Synchronous Optical Networking (SONET) market comprises various types, including Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) and Fiber Channel. WDM enhances bandwidth by enabling multiple signals to travel simultaneously over a single fiber using different wavelengths, improving efficiency and capacity. SONET standardizes data transmission over optical networks for reliable, high-speed communication. Meanwhile, Fiber Channel focuses on high-performance data transfer, mainly in storage area networks, ensuring low latency and high throughput. Together, these technologies drive advancements in optical networking solutions.</p></p>
