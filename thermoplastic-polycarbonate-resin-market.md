<p><p>The thermoplastic polycarbonate resin market is primarily categorized into extrusion grade and molding grade. Extrusion grade polycarbonate is designed for continuous processes, yielding sheets, films, and profiles with excellent clarity and impact resistance. Molding grade polycarbonate, on the other hand, is tailored for injection and blow molding applications, offering superior dimensional stability and mechanical strength. Both grades cater to diverse applications across industries such as automotive, electronics, and construction, driven by their unique processing requirements and performance characteristics.</p></p>
