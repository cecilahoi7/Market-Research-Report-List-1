<p><strong>The Polypropylene Fibre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polypropylene Staple Fibre</li><li>Continuous Fibre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene fiber market primarily consists of two types: polypropylene staple fiber and continuous fiber. Polypropylene staple fiber is short, discrete fibers used in applications like textiles, padding, and non-woven fabrics, offering lightweight properties and moisture resistance. In contrast, continuous fiber consists of long, continuous strands, commonly utilized in industrial applications, automotive interiors, and construction reinforcement due to its durability and strength. Both types cater to diverse industries, driven by demand for efficient, cost-effective materials.</p></p>
