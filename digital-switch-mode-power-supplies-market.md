<p><p>The Digital Switch-Mode Power Supplies (SMPS) market can be categorized into two main types: AC/DC and DC/DC converters. AC/DC converters transform alternating current (AC) from the mains into direct current (DC) for powering devices like computers and smartphones. In contrast, DC/DC converters adjust voltage levels within DC circuits, facilitating efficient power distribution in applications like electric vehicles and renewable energy systems. Both types are crucial for enhancing energy efficiency and reducing power loss in electronic systems.</p></p>
