<p><ul><li>Semi-Automatic Shrink Wrapping Machine</li><li>Fully Automatic Shrink Wrapping Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic shrink wrapping machine market comprises two main types: semi-automatic and fully automatic machines. Semi-automatic shrink wrapping machines require manual operation for some processes, such as loading products, while automation handles sealing and shrinking. These machines are ideal for small to medium production volumes. Conversely, fully automatic shrink wrapping machines automate the entire packaging process, from product loading to sealing and shrinking, making them suitable for high-volume production with reduced labor costs and increased efficiency.</p></p>
