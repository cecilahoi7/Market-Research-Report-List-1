<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic</li><li>Semi-Automatic</li><li>Manual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Die bonder machinery is categorized into three main types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully automatic machines offer high efficiency and precision, handling the bonding process with minimal human intervention, making them ideal for large-scale production. Semi-automatic machines strike a balance, requiring some manual input while enhancing productivity and consistency. Manual die bonders are operated entirely by personnel, offering flexibility and lower initial costs, but at the expense of speed and potential variations in quality.</p></p>
